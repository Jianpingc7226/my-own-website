What is Elimination?

Extinction refers to the death of the entire race caused by factors such as volcanic eruptions and food scarcity caused by natural disasters. Of course, this can also be artificial. The dodo bird discovered by humans in 1505 was easy to capture and hunted to extinction due to its huge size within two hundred years. Currently its specimens can only be seen in the Natural History Museum in Dublin, Ireland It is also the only dodo specimen left

Sixth species extinction

The sixth species extinction is the first species extinction that modern humans have really experienced or are experiencing. Due to geological changes and other reasons, organisms have experienced five species extinctions.

ButNow, due to the impact of human activities, the rate of species extinction is 1,000 times faster than natural extinction, and the earth is entering or has entered the sixth period of mass extinction.
