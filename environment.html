Biology needs to live in a specific environment, so its way of life is constantly constrained and affected by the environment. Facing the changing environment, most organisms can adapt and adjust themselves. For a biological race, genetic mutations have evolved and passed on to their offspring suitable genes that can help them survive. On the other hand, the environment will change due to the existence of living things. In the interaction of environmental and biological adaptation and evolution, life can be continued.
